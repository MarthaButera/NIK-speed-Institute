Who We Are

NIK Training Center is dedicated to empowering youth and professionals with practical skills in ICT, business, and technical fields. We believe in hands-on learning and provide real-world training experiences that prepare our students for today's digital economy.

Our institute is built on a foundation of passion, innovation, and excellence. Whether you're a beginner or advancing your knowledge, NIK is here to guide you through your IT journey with expert instructors and up-to-date training materials.

Our Vision & Values

  • Vision: To be a leading ICT training hub producing skilled, job-ready individuals in East Africa.
  • Mission: To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality training in Information Technology and business skills.
  • Core Values: Integrity, Innovation, Practical Learning, Community Impact, and Empowerment.
